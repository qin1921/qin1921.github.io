    6G的应用

    • Multisensory XR applications

      扩展现实，是AR/VR/MR的升级，需要引入QoPE来衡量，包括QoS（时延和速率）、QoE（主观感受）等

    • Connected Robotics and Autonomous Systems (CRAS)

    • Wireless Brain-Computer Interactions (BCI)

    • Blockchain and Distributed Ledger Technologies (DLT)

    驱动6G的技术趋势

    • 与5G不同，许多业务同时要求More bits， More spectrum, More Reliablity
    • 立体化的频谱和功率效率：bps/Hz/\(m^3\)/Joules
    • Emergence of Smart Surfaces and Environments:
    • 从集中化的大数据向海量的分布式的小数据迁移：motivates new machine learning and data analytics techniques that go beyond classical big data.
    • From Self-Organizing Networks (SON) to Self-Sustaining Networks：a self-sustaining network (SSN) can maintain its key performance indicators (KPIs), 持续地, under highly dynamic and complex environments stemming from the rich 6G application domains.
    • Convergence of Communications, Computing, Control, Localization, and Sensing (3CLS)
    • End of the Smartphone Era：可穿戴、XR、BCI

    6G的业务类型

    • Mobile Broadband Reliable Low Latency Communication（MBRLLC）：同时要求高速率、低时延和高可靠性
    • Massive URLLC：综合URLLC和mMTC的需求
    • Human-Centric Services 强调QoPE
